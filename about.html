About This Hero Page

Welcome to the Hero Profiles page!

This website was created to showcase some of the most iconic heroes from the Marvel Universe, including Thor, Iron Man, Hulk, Captain America, Black Widow, and Hawkeye.

Each hero has their own profile that shares key details such as their real name, special powers, origin story, weapons, and fun facts.

Whether you're a Marvel fan or just learning about superheroes, this page is here to inform, inspire, and celebrate these powerful characters.
